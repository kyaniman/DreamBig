Apply to Financial Aid and Register to College or University

You've Made It!


Congrats on making it to Cloud 9! 


In order to be seal the deal, you must apply to financial aid and register.


Applying to Financial Aid


Keep in mind that all colleges and universities are different. Please take a look at their website, or if you can visit the actual campus itself it would be best. 


Applying to financial aid can be confusing at times. I suggest applying to financial aid first before registering. Also, do this a few months before registering for classes. The first thing you need to be aware of is the deadlines. Please visit the college or university's site to see deadline dates for Financial aid. Once you know your deadline date, please visit fafsa.gov to apply to financial aid. You will need to spend time doing this. So, be sure to have at least 30 minutes to sit down and do this application. Once you submit your electronic application, your school will be notified that you have completed it. Usually schools will have an online status to show you the status of your application. Some show 'pending' or 'in progress'. All colleges and universities are different. 


Registering for College/University


When you are registering to the college or university of choice, keep in mind what they need you to do. Every college or university has a set list of things they need when going through the admissions process. For example:


  • Complete Apply Online link to register (Be sure to look at deadlines)
  • Provide transcripts of all institutions attended
  • Provide shot records (e.g. meningitis if needed)
  • Assessment Testing - depending on your state regulations
  • Visit with Academic Advisor for degree plan
  • Build a class schedule
  • Register
  • Pay for classes
