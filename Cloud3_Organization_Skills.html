Cloud 3 - Organization Skills: How do I become well-organized? 

Organization Skills

How do I become well-organized?

Organization skills can be done many ways:

  • Organizing your area by using binders, folders, paper clilps, staples, color coordination, etc.
  • Organizing yourself mentally by jotting down important information
  • Organizing yourself digitally by organizing your file folders, and knowing where digital materials are located


Once you find your own way of organizing your belongings, you will find yourself to be more efficient! You will be able to retrieve information when asked to do so. You will also feel less stressed out trying to find the thing you're trying to find. It's a win-win situation if you ask me.
