Cloud 4 - Preparation: How do I prepare? 

Preparation

How to Prepare Yourself


In order to prepare for college or university, you need to be on top of your game. You need to be able to not only handle the workload that they throw at you in school, but also the outside work you need to do. This includes preparing for the SAT and ACT. I suggest taking the practice tests of those first. Manage your time effectively and set time aside from your school work and other activities you have planned. Do not wait until the last minute to take the actual ACT and SAT. This will ultimately hurt your scores on them. I highly suggest taking the practice exams of those, but if you would like extra preparation for these exams, please take a look at the following sites:


  1. act.org


http://www.act.org/content/act/en/products-and-services/the-act/test-preparation/math-practice-test-questions.html?page=0&chapter=0


act.org has free online practice material. Their material ranges from English, Math, Reading, Science and Writing. Please go check this website out as a helpful way to practice for the ACT. 

   

    2.  4tests.com


https://www.4tests.com/sat#StartExam


4tests.com offers a variety of tests that you can take, but specifically speaking, the SAT practice test. On this website, you can also click the link in the blue box which will take you to another practice SAT exam. This is a very helpful source for those of you that want extra material.


There are also books that you can order online off Amazon.com. If you do not prefer self-study, then I suggest considering to hire a tutor. Tutors are a great way to have a set schedule on when to study, plus they are there if you need help.
