Cloud 8 - Researching Colleges and Universities: The benefits of doing research

Researching Colleges and Universities 

The Benefits of Doing Research

If you are able to research different colleges and universities, you will be able to feel more secure with your future. It will give you the peace of mind that you've been looking for. I know things in life are not easy, but with hardwork you dream big and achieve your goals.


If you need an idea on how to conduct research on which college or university is best for you, consider the following:


  • Is it affordable? Take a look at the cost of the college or university's cost per credit hour. This will give you an idea whether financial aid will be necessary or not.
  • Tip: If you are iffy on the major that you chose still, I highly recommend you take a look at community colleges. They are a great way to start, and offer excellent programs! Plus, they are inexpensive compared to university cost per credit hour.
  • Do they have my major at this college or university?
  • What do I have to do to get admitted to this college or university?  
  • Campus Life: What kind of events happen in this college or university? What is the culture like? Do you feel that you would fit in? 
  • Clubs and Organizations: Do they have extracurricular clubs and organizations that I can be part of? What do I need to do to be part of them?
